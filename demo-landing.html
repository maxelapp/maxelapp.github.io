Hello there!

I first started this project two years ago as a personal tool, since I was dissatisfied with the existing download accelerators for Mac OS X. I wanted something native, fast, and fun to use.

By using this demo, you've gotten a glimpse of what Maxel can do.

But development won't stop here. I have a roadmap for exciting features which get voted on and put into the App Store version of Maxel.


Why buy Maxel?

While the demo copy will continue to work, all further development will happen within the App Store version. By buying Maxel, you'll get:

  • Automatic updates for awesome features as soon they come
  • The warm fuzzy feeling from supporting indie devs
