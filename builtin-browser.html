Builtin Browser

Maxel's own view of the Internet.

Browse, Log In, Download

When you download a webpage within Maxel, it intelligently opens the Builtin Browser, which lets you browse to other pages or log in with your username and passwords to protected sites. Any downloads you initiate will be handled by Maxel.

Something's Not Working?

The Builtin Browser works with all secure download websites that support multipart downloads. If something does not work as you expect, email me (support@maxelapp.com) and I'll find out why.
