Thank You!

With your feedback, Maxel is better than ever. And this is only the beginning.


Hello there!

I first started this project two years ago as a personal tool, since I was dissatisfied with the existing download accelerators for Mac OS X. I wanted something native, fast, and fun to use.

Two years later I've finally arrived at something I'm proud to call version 1.0.

But development won't stop there. With your beta feedback (not too late to leave some!) I have a roadmap for an exciting v1.1, which will have features like Automatic download mode (no more need to specify a connection count) and a Safari extension.


Why buy Maxel?

While the beta copy will continue to work, all further development will happen within the App Store version. By buying Maxel, you'll get:

  • Automatic updates for awesome features as soon they come
  • The warm fuzzy feeling from supporting indie devs
